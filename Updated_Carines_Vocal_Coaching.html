About Carine

Hi, my name is Carine. I'm a graduate of Bachelor of Music from Excelsia College (formerly Wesley Institute). I specialize in Contemporary singing. I play gigs with my band at different wedding ceremonies and receptions. I'm also a session musician and songwriter. I've done multiple recording sessions for different albums, singles, projects, etc. Performed for choir gigs and as lead singer for global C3 conference, Christmas productions, lounge gigs, many end-of-degree recitals, etc. Other music experiences also include my sound engineering internship with professional sound engineers for about a year. Conducted and transcribed songs for choir.
