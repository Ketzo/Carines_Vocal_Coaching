How exciting!!! If you've booked your first lesson here is what you need to know.

What to Bring

For your lesson, please prepare two songs that best showcase your abilities and that present a challenge for you. Also, remember to bring water, a notebook, and a pen or pencil for note-taking.

How to Prepare

To get the most out of your lesson reflect on any specific areas you'd like to improve or questions you have, and feel free to bring a list with you.

Finding the Studio

Our studio is located at 47A Romney Crescent, Miller 2168. Look for the grey granny flat on the left side of the main house. We offer a private parking spot on the property—which is usually available, though it may occasionally be in use by previous students—and there is also street parking nearby. If you have any trouble finding a spot, please send me a text. 042 036 1088
