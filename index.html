About Carine

Hi my name is Carine. I'm a graduate of Bachelor of Music from Excelsia College (formerly Wesley Institute). I specialize in Contemporary singing. I've been a vocal coach since 2018. I perform at different gigs with my band at wedding ceremonies, receptions, functions, etc. I'm also a session musician and songwriter. I've done multiple recording sessions for different albums, singles, projects, etc. Performed for choir gigs and as lead singer for global C3 conference, Christmas productions, many end of degree recitals, etc. Other music experiences also include my sound engineering internship with professional sound engineers. I've also conducted and transcribed songs for choir.
